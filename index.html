Our Journey

OUR JOURNEY

Youth-driven and youth-focused organization.

Healing Dove Foundation was the fruit of the observations that were made by the founder and managing trustee Nitika Nagar when she was a young 18-year-old who was studying law at Symbiosis Law School, Pune.

  • Foundation
  • Overview
  • As a part of her academic sting, she was involved in writing a dissertation/thesis on the criminal justice system, focusing on the reformative and rehabilitative measures undertaken in India.
  • After spending some time in reform centres in Mumbai and Pune, she realized that the facilities established in these observation homes were inadequate to rehabilitate juveniles delinquents and bring them into mainstream society.
  • These revelations led to Nitika setting up the Healing Dove Foundation with the primary purpose of providing psychological, social and economic support to Juvenile delinquents in Mumbai.
  • Along with this, the foundation also started several mental health programs for the marginalized communities across India, Bangladesh, Nepal, Europe, Africa and other parts of Asia.
  • Now, the four-year-old organization is working with delinquents and various other marginalized groups such as specially-abled individuals, underprivileged women, underprivileged sportspeople, and the LGBTQ+ community, to name a few.
  • The aim is psychosocial, economic reformation and rehabilitation with the help of counselling, therapeutic activities, upskilling programs, and career development activities to the marginalized communities.
  • In a collaborative effort with ICHARS Support Foundation, Healing Dove Foundation launched Project Rainshine, Project MEHAK and Project YES.
  • They involve providing mental health assistance, capacity building programs, career-advancing endeavours, other training initiatives to youth, and organizations that are led by or working with youth.

What We Do?


Healing Dove Foundation empower mental health awareness by providing mental health support from trained practitioners who belong to our partner organization, awareness programmes, first aid mental health support training for the organization as well as the youth. We provide training for life and social skills and personality development. Our organization provide career counselling, vocational guidance and placement support.



Healing Dove Foundation aid our beneficiaries to get financial, management, induction, academic and industrial training by providing mentorship programs, ensuring holistic development for each individual.



Healing Dove Foundation follows a sustainable model by training organizations as well as individuals to be self-sufficient in providing relevant service to their beneficiaries and to be empowered through emotional and mental well being.
