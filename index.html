OUR JOURNEY

Youth-driven and youth-focused organization.

  • Foundation
  • Purpose
  • Aim
Healing Dove Foundation was the fruit of the observations that were made by the founder and managing trustee Nitika Nagar when she was a young 18-year-old who was studying law at Symbiosis Law School, Pune. As a part of her academic sting, she was involved in writing a dissertation/thesis on the criminal justice system, focusing on the reformative and rehabilitative measures undertaken in India for the wellbeing and psychosocial and economic transformation of juvenile offenders. After spending some time in reform centres in Mumbai and Pune, she realized that the facilities established in these observation homes were inadequate to rehabilitate juveniles delinquents and bring them into mainstream society.
These revelations led to Nitika setting up the Healing Dove Foundation with the primary purpose of providing psychological, social and economic support to Juvenile delinquents in Mumbai. Along with this, the foundation also started several mental health programs for the marginalized communities across India, Bangladesh, Nepal, Europe, Africa and other parts of Asia. Now, the four-year-old organization is working with delinquents and various other marginalized groups such as specially-abled individuals, underprivileged women, underprivileged sportspeople, and the LGBTQ+ community, to name a few.
The aim is psychosocial, economic reformation and rehabilitation with the help of counselling, therapeutic activities, upskilling programs, and career development activities to the marginalized communities. In a collaborative effort with ICHARS Support Foundation, Healing Dove Foundation launched Project Rainshine, Project MEHAK and Project YES. They involve providing mental health assistance, capacity building programs, career-advancing endeavours, other training initiatives to youth, and organizations that are led by or working with youth.

Mental Health Initiatives

All our current activities are categorized under the following projects


Project Rainshine

Project Rainshine, a collaboration between the ICHARS Support Foundation and the Healing Dove Foundation, focuses on training and mentoring various staff members at NGOs with skills to provide emotional and mental health first aid to their beneficiaries and connect them to mental health practitioners, trained by ICHARS, to offer advanced mental health support to marginalised communities.

Project YES

The youth face more stress today than they are equipped to handle, due to increased pressure and lack of mental health awareness. Project YES (Youth for Emotional Support) is a collaboration between the ICHARS Support Foundation and the Healing Dove Foundation, that, via online training programs, focuses on helping youth develop skills that enable them to manage their mental and emotional state effectively, and provide emotional first aid to their friends and family.

Learn More Donate Now

Project MEHAK

Project MEHAK (Mental and Emotional Health Awareness Through Knowledge) is a collaboration between the ICHARS Support Foundation and the Healing Dove Foundation, that focuses on breaking misconceptions, stigma, stereotypes, misinformation and prejudice associated with marginalised communities through live sessions, knowledge series and talk-it-out groups.


Learn More Donate Now

PROJECT DASH

Project DASH is a youth-to-youth training and skill development program that seeks to develop a long-term paradigm that aids in the emotional and economic rehabilitation of the neglected population across the world







Learn More Donate Now

PROJECT GURU

Project GURU strives to achieve a sustainable model that facilitates the psycho-social and economic reformation and rehabilitation of marginalised communities globally. The programme includes providing mental health assistance, capacity building programmes, career advancing endeavours and other training initiative to youth.
