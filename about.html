About Us

Home About Us

Youth-driven and youth-focused organization.<>

The Healing Dove Foundation (HDF) is a youth-led and driven organization that works towards achieving and establishing sustainable strategies that facilitate the psychosocial and economic reformation and rehabilitation of marginalized communities in India and across the world. Our vision is to equip the youth and the organizations led by or working with youth belonging to disadvantaged communities, with the necessary skills and expertise required to provide psycho-social and economic support.

Mission

Healing Dove Foundation strives to achieve a sustainable model that facilitates the psychosocial and economic reformation and rehabilitation of marginalized communities globally.


Identifying beneficiaries

The first step is the identification of our beneficiaries, i.e., the people that will benefit from our foundation. Our focus groups are youth belonging to marginalized communities.






Training

We will train our beneficiaries extensively, by employing our long-term sustainable programs, and equip them with the required skills to become self-sufficient, economical and independent in tackling their problems.





Conducting one-to-one sessions

We often conduct follow-up and mentoring sessions with our partner NGOs. Sometimes they face complex issues which require expert supervision and experience, and if intervention is required, our therapists and counselors conduct one-on-one sessions with them



Assessment

These interactions will be monitored, and a follow-up review and feedback mechanism will be devised to measure the effectiveness of our programs.







Collection and Analysis

The assessment data will be used to study the impact of our programs. We will analyze the results, and proceed to develop a blueprint of the strategies that work, or need improvement. This will help in revealing a pattern for the future, which can then be used to enhance the effectiveness of our programs.


Aims And Objective

We, at healing Dove Foundation, are dealing with problems through SMART objectives.


Sustainability

Our programs provide operational alternatives to our NGO partners aiming for long-term working systems with minimal or no dependency on resources outside the organization. This would enable them to cater to the psycho-social needs of their beneficiaries.


Mobilization

We educate and guide young people in leading programs and being agents of change as project managers, content creators, designers, legal support, web developers, technologists, policymakers, trainers, or administrators

Accessibility and Inclusivity

We work on providing our NGO partners with the tools needed to bring about psychosocial and economic reformation and rehabilitation within marginalized and disadvantaged communities, which include the underprivileged women and children, caregivers and caretakers, youths suffering from disabilities and terminal diseases, the LGBTQIA+ community, etc



Result Oriented

We train our partner NGOs to become self-sufficient by equipping them with the right skill sets and prepare them to develop strategies that will help them cater to the needs of their beneficiaries, even in times of crisis.



Time and Cost-effective

The interest of the organization lies in creating cost-effective measures for our beneficiaries. We train them with suitable skill sets so they can provide long-term mental health support to their beneficiaries at low costs.
