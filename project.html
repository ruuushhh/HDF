Project MEHAK


Project MEHAK (Mental and Emotional Health Awareness Through Knowledge) is a collaboration between the ICHARS Support Foundation and the Healing Dove Foundation, that focuses on breaking misconceptions, stigma, stereotypes, misinformation and prejudice associated with marginalised communities through live sessions, knowledge series and talk-it-out groups. Our goal is to equip all in dealing with issues faced in everyday life. We aim to initiate a dialogue with different members of marginalised communities, to help create mental health awareness and acceptance for all, and to discuss ways of coping with mental health and emotional concerns.

Aims and Objective

  1. Increasing awareness and reducing stigma surrounding diverse communities

  2. Decoding mental health issues experienced by individuals belonging to different communities

  3. Breaking myths with regard to prevalent stereotypes

  4. Decoding and spreading knowledge about techniques for emotional coping and mental health concerns



Processes

  • 01

    Live sessions with beneficiaries with emphasis on struggles faced with regard to their identities, how it affects their emotions and mental health, & understanding ways in which they deal with those struggles.

  • 02

    Talk it out groups where individuals share their experiences and emotions in a non-judgmental setting

  • 03

    Knowledge series consisting of awareness campaigns with the help of self-help videos & posts on social media handles and blogs & research articles on websites to promote emotional health awareness along with practical approaches for emotional self-care
