Project RAINSHINE


An initiative undertaken by ICHARS (Institute of Clinical Hypnosis and Related Sciences) Support Foundation and Healing Dove Foundation. The objective of this initiative is to reach out to other NGOs who are providing services to the marginalised sections of the society. The project aims at empowering the staff & employees of the other NGOs through training and mentorship in Emotional First Aid Support for their beneficiaries. Along with this the project aims at connecting mental health practitioners trained by ICHARS Support Foundation, with the other NGOs to provide mental health support to their beneficiaries.

Career counselling, Career Guidance & Career Mentoring

1) Conduct career aptitude assessments & help individuals identify their strengths

2) Conduct career counselling sessions to help individuals identify their weaknesses work on their strengths

3) Empower them to get equal opportunities to pursue academics and a career of their choice

4) Help in placements for young adults belonging to low income and marginalized backgrounds

Training other organizations in skills and expertise relevant for mental health and emotional support

1) Identifying and contacting organizations that need mental health training and support

2) Understanding the requirements of the organization with regard to training and mental health services needed

3) Delivering the base module of the training program along with a personalised training program designed to be suited for the best outcome with beneficiaries

Capacity building in Life Skills and Personality Development

1) Provide relevant soft skills, life skills and personality development training to the youths belonging to marginalised groups.

2) Academic and industrial training

3) Help them prepare for the world of work
